Genesis

Blender3D, Bambu Lab 3D Printer
PLA Plastic, PVA Glue, Tile Grout, Spider Plant
2025

Conceived as both a technical exploration and a symbolic work, Genesis embodies the tension between the exactness of machine production and the organic irregularites of nature, This functional sculpture replicates the contours of my own hand through digital fabrication while allowing the natural growth of the embedded spider plant to continously shape the form over time.

The sculpture began with a 3D scan of my hand and forearm. The scan was processed and refined in Blender to form an outstretched hand designed to hold a plant. The model was printed on a Bambu Lab PS1 3D printer and reinforced with a cement core to counterbalance the cantilevered form. The exterior surface was finished with a mixture of PVA glue and tile grout, producing a matte, stone-like texture.
