lovely

Blender, Daz Studios
2022

"lovely" was inspired by my friends' work in MODA, UChicago's Fashion Student Organization. Every year, MODA put on a fashion showcase featuring student-made garments and student models. I thought their work was incredibly cool and I wanted to try my own hand at it.

This project was my attempt at exploring texture using a procedural, digital process. It was created with Blender3D Geometry Nodes and the Shrinkwrap modifier. The base female mesh was exported from Daz Studio
