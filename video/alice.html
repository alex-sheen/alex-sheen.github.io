alice-03
Created with - Blender 2.92
Render Engine - Cycles
Photogrammetry AliceVision Meshroom

alice-03 was inspired by the work of Midge Sinnaeve. I photoscanned vines growing on the Kent Chemical Laboratory building located on the University of Chicago's campus. I then took a 3D base model of a human bust and unwrapped its mesh to a 2D plane. Lastly, I projected the photoscanned vines onto the unwrapped mesh by applying a Surface Deform modifier to have the vine texture conform to the 2D unwrapped mesh.
