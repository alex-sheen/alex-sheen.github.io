Opening the Rice Box

Created with - Blender 3.2.2, Polycam
3D Scan App - Polycam
Creation Date - 2023

This piece depicts how Asian-American artists are often confined to producing works that relate to the Asian-American experience.

I scanned my hand with Polycam and imported the mesh into Blender where I manually distorted its shape. I then printed the hand at UChicago's MADD Center. I placed heavy weights into the rice container to offset the weight of the hand and create the illusion that the hand is weightless and floating.
