Pose-Detection

TensorFlow.js
Created: 2022
This project used pre-trained pose-detection TensorFlow.js models to create an interactive art installation which was showcased in the lobby of UChicago's Media Arts, Data, and Design Center (MADD).

I set up a camera to catch the movements of passersby and projected the generated visual on to the wall using the MADD center's projector. Various parameters are tied to the position, distance, angle, and speed of the user's hands, feet, and arms. The installation accommodated multiple users.
