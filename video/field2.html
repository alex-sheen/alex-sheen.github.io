OpenEndedGroup Field2 / AR

These sketches were completed as assignments for my Augmented Reality Production course at UChicago. I created them using Field2, an AR program developed by my professor. This description is incomplete, please come back later for full details on each sketch!

For the final project, I partnered with Julia Koreman, an MFA student at UChicago, to translate their digital sculptures into AR. Julia's work explores hinged frames of differing dimensions and how their movement can reveal new interactions between the sculpture and the space it occupies.

Our AR piece plays with the common idea in AR of worlds inside of worlds. Our piece utilizes the Stencil Buffer to only draw the light gray sculptures when they are viewed through a black frame. Each frame is paired with a single gray object, but overlapping frames will reveal all of their respective objects.
