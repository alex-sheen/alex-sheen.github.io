1. BDCN
2. style2paints
3. Monet
4. Kandinsky

This sketch began with edge detection using BDCN on an image of Thresh, a character from the video game League of Legends. The resulting edge map was colorized with style2paints. The colorized image was then fed into style transfer neural networks for Monet and then Kandinsky
